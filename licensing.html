Licensing

The gaming industry has experienced unprecedented growth and expansion throughout the world. An essential foundation of the success that has led to such enormous growth has been the use of sound legal and regulatory structures. To operate and provide their services in Rwanda, gaming operators need to possess gaming license provided by Ministry of Trade and Industry through Gaming Regulatory Authority.

RGAA plays the role of bridge between gaming operators, regulatory authority and other stakeholders, therefore it advocates its members when it comes to license renewal, disciplinary matters and compliance.
