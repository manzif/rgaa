Membership

We are unified operators in Rwanda gaming industry including Casino, Sports Betting, Gaming Machines, iGaming and Lottery, working together to contribute to the growth of gaming industry in Rwanda.

With coordinated efforts, RGAA continues to seek ways to better represent the entire gaming industry, strengthening industry’s voice, reducing barriers to gaming workplace and innovation, enabling growth, and providing learning and networking opportunities with industry peers.

Benefits of Membership

  • Advocacy: Gaming business issue, advocate to combat illegal gambling, policies, regulations and tax
  • Reputation: Enhance your reputation by working with the sector’s experts and leading companies.
  • Influence: Contribute to RGAA’s work on national and international regulations which impact directly your gambling sector.
  • Networking: Participate to RGAA events and conferences.
  • Insight: Receive regular policy and regulatory updates on issues relevant to your business.
  • Information: Receive RGAA regular market news.
  • Professional trainings
