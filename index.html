    Welcome to Rwanda Gaming Association

    The Rwanda Gaming Association is the unique body of all gaming and gambling operators in Rwanda. It is a private sector initiative that operates under the auspices of Private Sector Federation of Rwanda.

    Become a member

    Advocacy

    RGAA plays the role of bridge between gaming operators, regulatory authority and other stakeholders, therefore it advocates its members when it comes to license renewal, disciplinary matters and compliance.

    Membership

    We are unified operators in Rwanda gaming industry including Casino, Sports Betting, Gaming Machines, iGaming and Lottery, working together to contribute to the growth of gaming industry in Rwanda.

    Mission

    The aims and objectives of RGAA are to advance the collective interests of its members, especially with regard to the Gaming Regulatory Authority, Rwanda institutions and to enhance the capability, common knowledge and status of individual members.

    Objectives

    Create a platform for operators of gaming activities in Rwanda.

    Represent and promote the interests of members in public and private sector, at national and international levels

    Provide advocacy and dialogue between members and different stakeholders.

    Raise the standards of service of the operators of gaming activities and grow their professionalism.

    Engage in social corporate responsibilities

    Activities

    01

    Represent members of the association in Private Sector Federation(PSF), Government and stakeholdersâ€™ programs and activities.

    02

    Develop and maintain relations with both national and international organizations that have direct or indirect similar objectives of the association.

    03

    Organize professional trainings of members and their employees on subjects of greater interest for their profession.

    04

    Facilitate the exchange of ideas and information among members.

    05

    Participate in elaboration of standards and regulations of gaming activities in Rwanda.

    06

    Carry out any activity likely to contribute to the achievement of our objectives.

    07

    Collect annual membership fees and other contributions.
